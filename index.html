Founder ● Lure Digital

Lure Digital began as a film production company in 2011, founded by Ky Schultz. We quickly found that companies wanted and required video content, but had a difficult time making their products look professional and artistic. Additionally, once a video was produced, businesses did not know how to utilize the final product so that it was able to stand out above the rest.
Stemming from these two needs, Lure Digital quickly grew into a creative agency that is designed to work with businesses who have video production and marketing demands, but lack an internal team. We work with your company to provide commercials & corporate videos, while also developing concrete social media strategies (specifically for YouTube) in order to truly allow your story to shine. We bring projects full circle by beginning with a concept and arriving at a final output, delivering handmade professional campaigns to you.
With a team of committed people, you will always talk to a person from Lure Digital directly, quickly, easily.
